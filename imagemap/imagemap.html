Dream Places to Visit

Explore my top five dream destinations. Click the highlighted areas on the map below, or scroll down to see why these places inspire me.

United States

Statue of Liberty

There is just something surreal about seeing the New York skyline in person. I'd love to take the ferry out there, see the sheer scale of the statue, and just feel the energy of a city that never sleeps.

Greece

The Parthenon

I've seen so many pictures of ancient Greek ruins, but standing right in front of them must be a totally different experience. I just want to walk around and take in a place that has been standing for thousands of years.

Italy

Tower of Pisa

It is one of those places you just have to see to believe. Beyond taking the classic, funny tourist photo, I genuinely want to see how this beautiful, flawed building has managed to stay standing for so long.

France

Eiffel Tower

It is a classic for a reason. I would love to grab some food, sit on the grass right beneath it, and just watch it light up at night. It feels like the ultimate travel milestone.

Rome

The Colosseum

You see it in movies and games all the time, but walking through the actual arches where gladiators used to fight sounds incredible. I want to feel the massive scale of this ancient arena for myself.

United Kingdom

Big Ben

There is something so iconic about the sound of Big Ben chiming in the middle of London. I'd love to walk across the bridge, hear the bells, and soak in that classic, cozy British weather.
