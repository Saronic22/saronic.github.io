About Me

I am a Computer Science student with a deep-rooted passion for technology that began early on, watching my father sell computers and eventually competing in PC assembly contests myself. My focus shifted from hardware to software after an award-winning Arduino robotics project in high school sparked a love for coding that defines my career path today. Currently, I specialize in software engineering and game development, channeling my creativity into projects like STRASHER and EaseUp while constantly expanding my technical skillset.
