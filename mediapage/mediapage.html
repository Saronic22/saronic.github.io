My Beats

Electronic / Synthwave

Nightcall

Artist: Kavinsky

Kavinsky is a French house artist whose production style heavily draws on 1980s film soundtracks. Nightcall is his signature track, featuring moody, vocoder-heavy vocals.

Lo-Fi Hip Hop

Luv(sic) Part 3

Artist: Nujabes feat. Shing02

Nujabes was a pioneering Japanese record producer celebrated for soulful, jazz-infused hip-hop beats. Luv(sic) Part 3 is a masterful blend of emotional piano loops.
